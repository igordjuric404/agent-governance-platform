# Shadow AI Discovery — Non-Technical Overview

## What Is Shadow AI Discovery?

**Shadow AI** refers to AI agents running in your organization that you don't know about — unauthorized, untracked, potentially dangerous. **Shadow AI Discovery** finds these hidden agents across your systems.

## Why It Matters

Without visibility:
- **Unmanaged risk** — You can't govern what you don't know exists
- **Compliance violations** — Unapproved AI processing sensitive data
- **Security blind spots** — Potential entry points for attackers
- **Resource waste** — Duplicate or unnecessary agents

## How It Works

Discovery scans for AI agents across:
1. **Process scanning** — Find agent processes on servers
2. **Configuration scanning** — Detect agent configs in codebases
3. **Repository scanning** — Find agent definitions in git repos
4. **Network scanning** — Identify agent endpoints

## Key Benefits

- Complete visibility into AI agents
- Risk scoring for discovered agents
- Automated compliance checking
- Inventory management

## The Bottom Line

Shadow AI Discovery ensures you know about every AI agent in your organization — so you can govern them properly and reduce unmanaged risk.
